About Me Who I Am !

Magdy Bakr I'm a web designer and developer based in Egypt. During the day I’m the front end developer at a local agency. During the evenings I spend my time working on freelance projects and building my own products. There’s nothing I enjoy more than designing and developing good websites for nice people. It really is that simple. I’ve spent many years trying to perfect what I do and while I’ll never be perfect, I do my best to come close. My specialty is full website strategy, design and development, making pixel magic in photoshop and turning it into beautiful, semantic HTML & CSS. My interests, however, extend beyond the web and I love helping people with branding, print design and marketing strategy. I even love making designs move by creating professional Websites.
